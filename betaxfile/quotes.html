The Quotes From The Red Carnival

People forget to tell and forget to remember the words they need. Not a thousands of words can tell a story or make us think when the words are empty. So we pour our thoughts, our feelings, our experience, age and emotion in those words. In some few lines , we bind them together and make quotes. These "Quotes" make rebellious wave in our hearts and revolves in our mind.

Here are some quotes by Norse Poet about love, hatred, happiness, grief, politics, society, peace and justice.
