A Story Of Half Street 
Neither can keep you near,
Nor can leave you alone.
Became a long-distance pedestrian
Walking in the middle of the street.
Neither can push you away,
Neither can meet.

That's you and me
In a story of half street.
_______End_______

An enrising sun always falls at the edge of the window
