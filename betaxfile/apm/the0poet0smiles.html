Written by Norse Poet

The Poet Smiles 
A sheet of fine fog
Caress the grass,
I don't know if people will return
Autumn comes back.
If the roses were not red in the spring
If not for the fragrant aroma,
But what would be the beginning of a story?
This dilemma makes me smile and this mind floats...
_______End_______

An enrising sun always falls at the edge of the window
