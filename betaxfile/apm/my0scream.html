The Poems from the Green Lief Diary

Written by Norse Poet

My Scream 
Wrong cities full of rubbish,
All spirits, wicked,
Hides the real look,
The smile on their face is still false.

In the crowd of thousands, I drowned
No one cares
Save me, I'm here
Here I am, speechless ...

I'm tired, I'm looking for you
Walking down this road.
I wonder, how far?
But again the day is over

Today in the desert of zero sapphires,
Black and white
That's why I raised my hand,
Will you give me completion?
_______End_______

An enrising sun always falls at the edge of the window
