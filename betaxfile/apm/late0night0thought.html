Late Night Thought 
Beloved, my beloved, I will go to your house.

I make mistakes in math while reading in the evening.
I was in tenth grade then I was sixteen then
I went to see her ,
Hiding under the bridge

Precious after so many days
Honestly, I still remember those things
You said amny things, said who was your lover?
I only have one day with you and you have him.
I saw that wonderful light under the bridge,
I admit that the two of them had a good day
She covered my eyes and filled my chest,
I came home and said goodbye to them.
At night I weny to sleep in the first floor room
The moonlight fell on the bed leaves on the floor
The sister who was after me was on the sidelines
I don't know who she was with today
Today, what will happen tomorrow,
Saturn in tomorrow's hope

What if I'm a spoiled boy!

Beloved,
