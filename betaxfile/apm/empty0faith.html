The Poems from the Green Lief Diary

Written by Norse Poet

Empty Faith 
I don't know why sometimes on the doorstep
Unbearably restless time passes

I don't know why it happens sometimes
Bored time spent in indifferent proximity

I don't know why sometimes my color is emotional
Lives clear time with great affection

All morning- noon evening- night
Devotion is the heavenly right

Constantly breathing
Spend time in incredible faith.
_______End_______

An enrising sun always falls at the edge of the window
