Forbidden Love 
At the end of a thousand days of rigorous penance,
Goddess called me one day!
Asked the ascetic.
What do you want? '
Bowing my head I said,
"It was What I wanted in a thousand days of austerities to you!"

She growled,
Said in a calm voice, 'It will never happen! human,
I am giving you the most beautiful heaven,
You will get whatever you want there,
Everything there is yours.
I begged, "Will I get her there if I want?"

Goddess was silent for a while
And said, 'Raise your head and look at me.'
I looked up and was shocked!
I asked,
So whom  I loved  for a thousand days?
The goddess said "It was me!"

The goddess came to humanity for a thousand days,
ven fell in love with a young man with a weak human form!
I loved and cared her as my goddess,
Even as in mortal form, despite knowing her divinty
I said, "Will you give me whatever I want without you?"
Goddess said, yes.
Then I thought calmly for a while and said,
Give me death, O Goddess.
This soul is already dead, let this body rot,
Whatever heaven or hell I get,
Atleast I can stay close to you!

The goddess gave me a boon,
She felt grief and sad!
Was there water in the corner of her eye then?
I don't understand,
I just stared .....
_______End_______

An enrising sun always falls at the edge of the window
