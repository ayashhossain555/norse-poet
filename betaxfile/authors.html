The Author of Norse Poet

Ayash Hossain Chowdhury

He is the main author of the "Norse Poet". He is the creator of "Norse Poet's Community" and website designer & developer of "Norse Poet". Currently, he is completing his undergraduation programme at BRAC University's Computer Science and Engineering department. He is born in 2001 and he is from Noakhali District of Bangladesh. Writing was always his hobby and also cooking, drawing and singing just like any other person. After completing his higher secondary in 2019, this young poet was confused what to do next. As his father suggestion, he started taking preperation for the admission in medical college. Due to many circumstances he didn't got chance in medical colleges. After that it became tough for him to get admitted in any public universities. It was great struggle for him to meet the expectation of his family, close ones, relatives and society. But he stood on his feet in his hard times and didn't give up. He got admitted at BRAC university in CSE department. Now he is persuing his dream to get scholarship for higher study at abroad and become a great programmer. In his hard time poems, writings and books never left him. So, he build this website and build this community for everyone. The author wanted to share his writings with us to give others a little hope through his works or maybe a poem over a morning tea cup...

If anyone want to know more about him or have anything to tell or ask the author, please contact the author.
Thank You
