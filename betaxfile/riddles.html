The Riddles To The Yellow Rose

Riddles are mysterious questions which make us thoughtful and thrilled with joy. These riddles also lead us to some aspects that make us realize some subjects and give us knowledge. So we should solve riddles, "Riddles are always meant to solve!".

Here are some riddles by Norse Poet about love, hatred, happiness, grief, politics, society, peace and justice.

Written by Norse Poet 

I sleep in dreams and dream of reality
Then tell me where I am ?
Am I in dreams or in reality?

Ans: If the dream becomes much real,
We start to live in that dream,
Then people call us crazy and fool.
And if the reality starts to look like dream,
Then we call it love.So think, where are you now??
